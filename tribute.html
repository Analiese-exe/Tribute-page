Frida Khalo

The Mexican artist known for her self-portraits and works inspired by Mexican culture.

  • 1925: Kahlo began to paint while recovering from a near-fatal bus accident
  • 1927: Kahlo joined the Mexican Communist Party
  • 1929: Kahlo married fellow Mexican artist Diego Rivera
  • 1938: Kahlo had her first solo exhibition at the Julien Levy Gallery in New York, arranged by surrealist artist André Breton
  • 1939: Kahlo had another solo exhibition in Paris. The Louvre purchased a painting from Kahlo, The Frame, making her the first Mexican artist to be featured in their collection
